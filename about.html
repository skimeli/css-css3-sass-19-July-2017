What is mursik?

Mursik is a special drink made from milk mostly common among the kalenjin people in kenya.

Why the festival?

Mursik festival is meant to celebrate the mursik tradition which is highly valued and to also preash peace

Mursik festival was established in 2014 by the legend atheletes in kenya that came up together to see the success of the festival.
